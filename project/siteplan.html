HYPEthread Site Plan

Course: WDD 131 | Individual Website Project

Site Name

HYPEthread is a bold, fashion-forward brand name that merges the excitement of “hype” culture with “thread” as a casual term for clothing. It captures the essence of modern streetwear and contemporary style while remaining simple and memorable.

Site Purpose

HYPEthread is a showcase of streetwear culture, fashion highlights, and trend-based apparel. The site will serve as a digital hub to explore the latest looks, must-have items, and connect with the culture behind emerging fashion styles. It may also include product previews and a form to join the HYPEthread community.

Scenarios

  • What are the top trending streetwear styles this season?
  • Where can I join the HYPEthread mailing list or learn more about upcoming drops?

Site Map

The website will have the following main pages:

  • Home – Introduction to the HYPEthread vibe and featured styles.
  • About – Style inspirations and curated fashion updates.
  • Products – Brand mission, culture, and creative direction.
  • Contact – Contact form and social/community links.

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #1A1A1D – Deep Black (backgrounds and strong contrast)
  • Accent Color: #6A0DAD – Royal Purple (highlights, headings, buttons)
  • Secondary Color: #D3D3D3 – Light Grey (backgrounds, light text areas)

Typography

  • Heading Font: Montserrat – sharp, bold sans-serif for impactful headers
  • Body Font: Poppins – clean, fashionable, and readable for body content
